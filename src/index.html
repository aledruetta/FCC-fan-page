Linus Torvalds

The man who came in from the cold

The genius behind Linux

Linus Benedict Torvalds born in Helsinki, Finland, in December 28, 1969. Linus is the creative genius behind the Linux Kernel.

Android, Chrome OS, and the almost entire Internet infrastructure spin around his creature.

He also created the distributed revision control system GIT.

For more information you can read about Linus at Wikipedia.
